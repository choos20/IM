About SK CONNECT

Empowering the youth and community through transparency, engagement, and innovation.

Mission

To create meaningful programs and activities that empower youth in our barangay and foster community development.

Vision

To be a progressive, inclusive, and transparent Sangguniang Kabataan organization that inspires growth and participation in the community.

Goals

  • Encourage youth participation in governance and social programs.
  • Promote sports, education, and cultural events.
  • Ensure financial transparency and accountability.
  • Foster environmental and social responsibility.
